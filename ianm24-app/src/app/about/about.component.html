About This Website

The idea of this website is to act as a sort of blog for projects I work on. Hobby projects tend to be sporadic so new posts here will be also, but I will try to give a good understanding of the thought processes and challenges that I come across during such projects. I may gradually make posts for past projects but no guarantees.

About Me

My name is Ian McDowell and I make programs occasionally. I am a Sophomore dual Computer Science/Mathematics major at the University of South Carolina Columbia. I currently work for the Center for Digital Humanities at UofSC, primarily helping with the development of the Victorian Lives and Letters Consortium (VLLC) websites like the Carlyle Letters Online.

I tend to focus on Python for hobby projects, but my work has gotten me working with the MEAN stack so I figured I would share some of that web development skill here, even with the limited backend of Github Pages.
